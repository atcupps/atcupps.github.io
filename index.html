Work.

Professionally, I have worked for three summers as an intern at Google (hyperlink to their webpage), a web search engine company based in California. This year, I worked on the Gmail CSE (client-side encryption) team on internal server migrations. Last year I worked on experiments to modify network behavior in containerized infrastructure for Fuchsia OS development. In 2023, I worked on the internationalization libraries team to make our technologies more accessible to people in other countries and speaking other languages, specifically working on algorithms to convert dates in an open-source i18n library ICU4X to and from the Chinese and Korean lunar calendars.

Nonprofessionally, I am the creator of Jupiterp, an open-source website built to improve the process of planning course and section registration for fellow students at the University of Maryland. As of November 2025, over 20,000 students (including 4,000 monthly) have used Jupiterp to help plan their courses!

I was also previously the Director of Partnerships at Startup Shell, a student-run non-profit organization dedicated to providing students who want to work on cool projects, small businesses, or startups with resources and a network to do so.

Before all this, I was a proud employee of Jersey Mike's Subs and Sandwiches. I recommend: #13 Original Italian, Mike's Way with the oil and vinegar on immediately after the lettuce (otherwise it splashes off the tomatoes), pickles, and banana peppers.
