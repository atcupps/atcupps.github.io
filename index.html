> About Me

I am a student at the University of Maryland currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science double major in Computer Science and Astronomy. I enjoy studying data science, machine learning, internationalization, and cosmology.

This past summer (2023), I had the amazing opportunity to work at Google as a STEP Intern on the internationalization (i18n) libraries team by contributing to ICU4X, an open-source i18n library written in Rust with a focus on efficiency and reduced binary size to improve accessibility from low-resource environments.
